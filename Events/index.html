MIX AND MATCH

Date will be updated soon

Set a standard to your concrete mix strength! The event tests the concrete mix design skills. It requires an overview of how cement,sand and water interact in a concrete mix. The competent requires knowledge to use, produce, place and finish concrete cubes, to come up with the best compressive strength possible. Concrete mix of specific grade is designed by the participants and after 7 or 28 days of casting and curing 3 cubes, it is tested for compressive strength in Compression Testing Machine.
The competition consists of total four rounds :-
First Round : Preliminary Test
Second Round : Viva
Third Round : Group discussion and presentation
Fourth Round : Practical
The questions will be based on civil engineering

Event Co-ordinators :
Anujith K Babu : +91 9947541230

NTHROPOMETRIST

Date will be updated soon

Anthropometry is the method of taking measurements with human tools and abilities. During the events,contestants will come across a variety of specimens whose dimensions are to be uncovered using spacial visualisation ability to mentally manipulate two dimensional and three dimensional figures with simple cognitive tools. This is a creative activity involving imagination, intuition and deliberate choice. After qualifying Preliminary written test, participants will be permitted to the final round of the event. The team that finds out the dimensions of the given object with greatest precision will be declared the winners.
